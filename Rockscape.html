Rockscape


Rockscape is a rock band from the crooks of VIT University which makes rock music to
escape the tensions of daily life and enjoy an ephimeral euphoria that comes while playing it.
Khushagra Anand on rhythm guitar and Shivansh Nagi on bass guitar, being the founder of the band,
has led it all the way from the beginning till now to reach the pinnacle. With the powerfull vocals of
our lead singer Tushar Raghuvanshi and with the guitar patches heavier than a rock of our lead guitarist Akaash Dhamija,
we won us several competitions around the neighbouring Universities. But alas, it is all about the rhythm because,
I play drums here! XD

We've been producing music for last two years and have come up with 3 original compositions and several cover.
The links to our youtube channel as well as out facebook page are mentioned below. Hope you would like out music!
