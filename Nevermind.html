Nevermind


Nevermind is the only hard rock band from the Music Club of VIT University. This band is inspired by some
of the legendary bands of all time like Led Zeppelin, Guns and roses etc. The powerful vocals, just like
Robert Plant, of our lead singer Saptarishi Mukhopadhyay, has led its way from nowhere to being one of the
best band in the college. The frazzling guitar tones of our guitarists Maitray Sharma and Arunabh Pushilal
can literally turn stadiums into the war zones of the moshpit. An incredible bass player Josh Neutron with the support of
drummer, Me, fills all the gaps by his soothing but heavy tones and playing techniques.

We've been making music for last 2 years and during the time, we have come up with two original compositions.
The links to our own compositions are mentioned below. Hope you like our music :)
