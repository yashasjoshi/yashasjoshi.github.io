Design

As a designer, I have a clear idea of how I want my product to work and how I would want my audience to interact with my product but usually, it is not that easy. Sometimes I forget to put myself as a user, therefore after going through the whole sketching procedure, a quick prototype is the right way to validate the design.

I found out that getting real feed back from the users is kinda of a challenge and usually they do not share their honest point of view with the design team or the developing team. In this case, I myself check the product several times and fix the bugs because in my opinion, it is always better to make some effort on our own rather then hurting the feelings of those who are working on the project.

When running a test like this it's better to be part of the users taking the test and put attention to the comments they provide and over all and the most important is to put yourself in the user perspective.
